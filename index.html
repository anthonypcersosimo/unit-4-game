Crystal Collector!

Instructions:

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals on the page. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the number that is randomly generated. You lose the game if your total score exceeds the random number. We are looking for a perfect match here!

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on the crystal image.

On each restart/new game, the number associated with each crystal will change. ~GLHF~
